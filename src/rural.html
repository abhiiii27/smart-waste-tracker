How to Make

Biogas Production

  1. Mix cow dung with water
  2. Put the mixture in a biogas tank
  3. Wait 20–30 days
  4. Methane gas will be produced

Banana Fiber Extraction

  1. Cut banana stem into strips
  2. Scrape fibers gently
  3. Wash and dry fibers
  4. Spin into yarn or rope

Mushroom Farming (Rice Husk)

  1. Sterilize rice husk
  2. Add mushroom spawn
  3. Maintain humidity
  4. Harvest after 20 days
